RRR

Luka Chuppi

The Hangover

Mean Girls

Cady joins a new public school and befriends Janis and Damian. They warn her to avoid the Plastics, a group led by Regina, but things get worse when she falls in love with Aaron, Regina's ex-lover.

The Princess Diaries

A clumsy, shy teen discovers that she is the princess of Genovia, a small European state. Thereafter, she must prove to her grandmother that she is worthy of her title.

Mary Kom

M.S. Dhoni

Lion

Steve Jobs
